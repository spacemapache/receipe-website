Protein Banana Bark

Prep time: 5 Mins, Set time: 1 Hr

Ingredients

  • 3-4 sliced bananas
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • Flakey sea salt

Instructions

  • Place the banana slices on aparchment lined baking tray to create a base bark layer.
  • In a small bowl, mix together the peanut butter and protein powder.
  • Spread the peanut butter on top of the banana base, creating a thin even layer.
  • Place the chocolate chips in a smallbowl and melt in the microwave for 30-60 seconds.
  • Pour on top of the peanut butter and spread into an even layer.
  • Top with a dash of flakey sea salt.
  • Place in the freezer for about an hour to set before enjoying. Keep stored in a ziplock in the freezer.
